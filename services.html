PortNus

Solution

PortNus: is one of our system integration product which meet the need of marine traffic and water ways monitoring system. The most important functions of a PortNus as those related to:

  • Safety of Life at Sea
  • Safety of Navigation
  • Efficiency of Vessel Traffic Movement
  • Protection of the Marine Environment
  • Supporting Maritime Security
  • Supporting Law Enforcement
  • Protection of adjacent communities & infrastructure
  • Manage, control and record all vessel movement on the port water ways

What we delivered:

PortNus, the integrated marine traffic and waterways monitoring system that is the reliable system configurated with Furuno navigational equipment among other class A AIS unit, weather sensor, radar and integrate with comprehensive monitoring and controlling dashboard. The configuration is customizable for scalability both of system and infrastructures.

The PortNus software were developed by PT Jawara’s R&D team which hold the Letter of Appointment and Recommendation Letter from PT. FURUNO ELECTRIC INDONESIA as the most compatible integrated software for Furuno equipment. We are designed the system to all operational constraint of the marine used standard with basic consideration as follow:

  • Utilizes Commercial Off the Shelf (COTS) hardware to reduce costs and improve project delivery timescales to the full benefit of the customer
  • The system is very user-friendly offering the operator the opportunity to view their maritime domain on an Electronic Navigation Chart rather than a standard display which requires fine tuning and a high level of expertise and experience to decipher the picture presented
  • Sensors and other module are ‘plug in’ capabilities and can be added to suit operational need
  • The flexibility of the configuration and customize dashboard as customer requirement

PortNus 1st Feature: Maritime Software Development Kit (ECDIS Kernel) installed into PortNus which provide Electronic Nautical Chart (ENC) that give us the complete and updating marine navigational chart information

PortNus 2nd Feature: Radar, Sensor and Database Integration provides the ultimate experiences for the Marine Traffic and Water-ways operator with comprehensive functionalities and toolbars

PortNus 3rd Feature: Data Summary Dashboard can be customized based on Operator’s Need will consist of any information that be needed to make a quick decision by operators

PortNus 4th Feature: It can be customized based on Company Business Process, we can help defining and translating a company business process analysis then implementing it into the solution by our expert team
